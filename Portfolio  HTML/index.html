I’m Prajwal Jamkattel – a frontend developer who thrives at the intersection of logic and creativity. With 1+ years of hands-on experience, I specialize in crafting responsive interfaces that users enjoy interacting with, not just use. My toolkit spans HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript (including React and TypeScript), complemented by backend exposure through Node.js and PostgreSQL.

My journey began with a CIT Certificate IV in IT, where I discovered the magic of turning code into visual experiences. Now pursuing a Bachelor’s in IT, I pair academic rigor with real-world application – whether building full-stack prototypes, optimizing website performance, or experimenting with Material UI’s latest components.

What sets my work apart? A dual focus on precision and empathy. I don’t just write code; I architect solutions that consider accessibility needs, cross-device behavior, and long-term maintainability. When I’m not debugging or exploring new frameworks, you’ll find me dissecting UX patterns on popular apps or contributing to open-source projects.

I’m currently seeking opportunities to collaborate with teams that value clean code and cleaner user experiences. Let’s connect if you’re passionate about building the web’s next generation of intuitive, inclusive applications.
